# Pre-Trade Checklist

A systematic checklist to complete before entering any trade. Based on professional trading methodology.

## Market Analysis

- [ ] Identified current market phase (accumulation/markup/distribution/markdown)
- [ ] Checked higher timeframe trend direction
- [ ] Identified key support and resistance levels
- [ ] Analyzed volume profile at key levels
- [ ] Checked for any major news or economic events

## Trade Setup

- [ ] Clear entry signal based on trading system
- [ ] Entry price identified
- [ ] Stop-loss level defined (based on structure, not arbitrary)
- [ ] Take-profit target(s) identified
- [ ] Risk-to-reward ratio is at least 1:2

## Risk Management

- [ ] Position size calculated (max 1-2% risk per trade)
- [ ] Total portfolio exposure within limits
- [ ] No revenge trading or overtrading
- [ ] Not trading during high-impact news (unless intentional)

## Psychology Check

- [ ] Emotionally calm and focused
- [ ] Not trading out of boredom or FOMO
- [ ] Accepting of potential loss before entering
- [ ] Following the trading plan, not impulse

## Final Confirmation

- [ ] All boxes checked above
- [ ] Trade logged in journal before entry
- [ ] Ready to execute
