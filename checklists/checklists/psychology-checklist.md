# Trading Psychology Checklist

Daily psychological assessment for traders. Complete this before your trading session.

## Emotional State Assessment

- [ ] Slept at least 7 hours last night
- [ ] No major personal stress or distractions today
- [ ] Feeling physically healthy and alert
- [ ] Not under influence of alcohol or medications affecting judgment

## Mental Readiness

- [ ] Reviewed trading plan for today
- [ ] Clear goals for the session (not just "make money")
- [ ] Accepting that losses are part of the process
- [ ] Not trying to recover yesterday's losses

## Cognitive Biases Check

- [ ] Not anchored to a specific price or outcome
- [ ] Open to being wrong about market direction
- [ ] Not overconfident from recent wins
- [ ] Not fearful from recent losses

## Discipline Markers

- [ ] Will follow stop-loss rules without exception
- [ ] Will not increase position size after losses
- [ ] Will take breaks if frustrated or tired
- [ ] Will stop trading after reaching daily loss limit

## Red Flags (Do NOT trade if any apply)

- [ ] Feeling desperate to make money
- [ ] Angry at the market or specific trades
- [ ] Trading to prove something to someone
- [ ] Unable to accept a losing trade today

## Session End Review

- [ ] Followed the trading plan
- [ ] Managed emotions effectively
- [ ] Learned something new
- [ ] Ready to improve tomorrow
